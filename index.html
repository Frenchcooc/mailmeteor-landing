Get started

Send your first mail merge campaign with Mailmeteor.

Add contacts in Gsheets

Start by creating a Google sheets containing your recipients list, or by duplicating our example sheet.

  • Each column represents one personalized tag in your mail
  • Be sure to add one column with all your recipients emails
  • You can add as many columns as you want

Write a draft in Gmail

In Gmail, compose the draft that will serve as the basis for the campaign.

  • Add personalized tags in your draft, like this : {{personalizedtag}}
  • The tags names must corresponds with your columns name in Gsheet. Avoid uppercase or spacing in your tags names.
  • You can go very far in the personalization, add as many variables as you want

Preview emails and send

Once you're done writing your emails, you're all set to test and send.

  • Mailmeteor allows you to preview each emails that will be sent
  • Check your emails with different recipients.
  • After sending, track your campaigns stats right in your Gsheet

FAQs

Is Mailmeteor free?

You can send up to 100 emails a day with Mailmeteor for free. If you want to benefit from premium features and unlock your sending limit, you can upgrade your account on our paid plans.

How is Mailmeteor different from other mailing solutions?

Contrary to email marketing softwares (like Mailchimp) Mailmeteor lets you send emails right from your Gmail inbox. As if you actually write each and every emails. It means better email deliverability, less spams and way better opening rate! You could also compare Mailmeteor to other mail merge extensions for Gmail. Mailmeteor is different because it's built on modern technologies, approved by Google, hence Mailmeteor is way faster. Also, you can get twice more free quota than the traditional solutions.
