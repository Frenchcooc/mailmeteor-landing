FAQs

Is Mailmeteor free?

You can send up to 100 emails a day with Mailmeteor for free. We've doubled the free quota compared to existing mail merge solutions. If you want to benefit from premium features and unlock your sending limit, you can upgrade your account on our paid plans.

How many emails can I send per day with Mailmeteor Premium?

To help prevent spam and keep accounts safe, Gmail limits the number of emails you can send per day:

  • G Suite accounts have a limit of 1,500 recipients per day
  • Regular Gmail accounts have a limit of 500 recipients per day
Mailmeteor Premium allows you to send personalized emails up to these limits imposed by Gmail.
Read more about Google's email quotas policy for Gsuite or for Gmail users.

How is Mailmeteor different from other mailing solutions?

Mailmeteor lets you send email campaigns from your Gmail inbox, contrary to email marketing softwares - like Mailchimp or Sendgrid. For you recipients, it is as if you actually wrote each and every emails. It means better email deliverability, less spams and way better opening rates!

You can also compare Mailmeteor to other mail merge extensions for Gmail - like Yet another mail merge or Gmass. Mailmeteor is different in three ways:
  • Stellar user experience - it's mail merge made easy
  • Affordable pricing - starting from $2 a month
  • Privacy first - Mailmeteor never requires access to your email data while other add-ons (like YAMM or Gmass) can read, modify and delete your emails.

Can I send email from space?

Mailmeteor works anywhere you can access internet, so technically you should be able to send emails from space. If you manage to do it, please send us a picture. It is something we would love to tweet about!

How to cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at anytime right from the addon.

To do so, open a Google spreadsheet, click on the "Add-ons" menu, then select "Mailmeteor" > "My account". From there, follow the instructions and the subscription will be cancelled immediately after.

If you cancel before the end of your subscription period, you will still be able to use your Mailmeteor premium account (up to the last day of the subscription 🛰)
